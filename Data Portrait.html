Data Portrait

How many people per day do I text?

Motivation

I reflected on my online communication and visualized how many people per day I chat online. I got data for a week.

Reflection

It was interesting to know I chat with large number of people from Friday to Sunday compared to weekdays. Also, I thought I talked with more people online but it turned out the number is smaller than I expected. I gained the clearer understanding of myself by visualizing data. I don't think I lost anything since the data is simple enough and I didn't feel I had to omit any part of the data I collected.
