Experimental Clock

Meditation

Motivation

I was going to my first yoga class in the U.S. right before finishing this assignment so I drew my favorite yoga pose. In addition to yoga, I had 2 other ideas. My design sketches are below. Sketch 1: Scenery with the sun (from sunrise to sunset) If I close my eyes, time goes more slowly, else, time goes by in normal speed (if/else function and map function) Sun moves according to seconds and the color of sky changed accordingly Sketch 2: Walk If I walk from left to right in the upper half of the canvas, then the footsteps accumulate in the bottom half of the canvas. Background color changes according to seconds I chose Sketch 3 because I liked how the circle represents my breathing and how the synchronization between my arm movements, breathing, and my internal sense of time is depicted

Reflection

It was valuable learning experience to understad how to create a natural transition from a static image to a dynamic one that changes over time. I think I could make the coding simpler so will try to make it simpler in the next time.
