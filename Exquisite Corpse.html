Do I look pretty?

Motivation

Since I got two woman's picture from my partners, I tried to describe both the elegance and strongness that women have. Here is the background story: A rabbit is dressing up. It's her first time to try an elegant skirt, climing up stairs to become an adult. She keeps wearing her favorite cat T-shirt. When we clicked the screen, cats appear on the screen, which describes her confidence that she looks good in her favorite T-shirt.

Reflection
